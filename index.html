Maltese

Maltese are very lively and playful dogs.

Hypoallergenic: Yes
Life Span: 12-15 years
Height: 8-10 inches
Weight: 6.6-8.8 lbs
Origin: Mediterranean Basin
Temperament: Playful, Affectionate and Fearless

Maltese are very energetice and fearless small dogs. They enjoy going out and being close with people. Maltese are very sweet when being close to the people that they trust. They show their love by hugging people's legs or laying down and turning their tummies up. Although maltese are very sweet most of time, they may occasionally be snappish with small children, and bark constantly when feeling insecure.
